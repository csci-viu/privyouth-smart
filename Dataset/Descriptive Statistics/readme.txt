This supplementary file, descriptive_statistics.xlsx, contains item-level descriptive statistics for the study variables. Each row corresponds to a survey item; the spreadsheet reports the following fields:

items: item identifier/label

vars: variable code/name

n: valid (non-missing) sample size for the item

mean, sd: arithmetic mean and standard deviation

median: median

trimmed: trimmed mean

mad: median absolute deviation

min, max, range: minimum, maximum, and range

skew, kurtosis: skewness and kurtosis

se: standard error of the mean

File: descriptive_statistics.xlsx
Format/Requirements: Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) or compatible spreadsheet software.
Use: Supports verification of descriptive results reported in the manuscript and enables reproducible interpretation of distributional properties at the item level.
